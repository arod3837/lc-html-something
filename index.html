Family Vacations to Florida

Some of my favorite memories were created with my family during our vacations together. A state we like to visit is Florida. I have been fortunate enough to go a few times and experience different areas. Two of my favorite places we visited are Flagler Beach and New Smyrna Beach.

  • Flagler Beach, FL - 2017
  • New Smyrna Beach, FL - 2018

Flagler Beach, FL

In August 2017, my family and I set off on a 14 to 15 hour journey to Flagler Beach, located along Florida's Atlantic Coast between Daytona Beach and St. Augustine. Flagler has many waterfront restaurants, shops, and natural parks. On this trip we were able to explore some of the parks, played beach games, relaxed a lot, and visited Castillo de San Marcos in St. Augustine. Castillo de San Marcos is the oldest stonework fort in the U.S. Over 315 years old, Castillo de San Marcos is a large Spanish stone fortress built to protect and defend Spain's claim in the New World.

New Smyrna Beach, FL

In July 2018, my family and I flew into Daytona Beach International Airport then drove about 30 minutes to New Smyrna Beach. New Smyrna Beach is located on Florida's Central Atlantic Coast. This beach town can be known for its locally owned restaurants along the beachside, excellent surfing conditions, and is one of three locations in Florida where beach driving is allowed. Although I did not attempt to surf, I was able to eat at a few of the mom-and-pop styled restaurants and cruise along the beach.
